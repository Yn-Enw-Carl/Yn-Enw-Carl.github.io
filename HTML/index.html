Welcome to Introversation

Introversation is a new social media application designed to appeal to introverts in a bid to get them interacting and socializing with other people again. The intention is to provide a safe envrionment which will enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking at some capacity.

Target

Our target is to make a self-sustainable application which sees the development of introverts into confident people.

Puzzle

Finding a good friend or parter is pretty hard; it's all about finding the right person. This application will hopefully take away the stress and provide a relaxed place for said partner.

Interests

Have a shared interest in music? Like Led Zeppelin? Then connect with people with the same or similar interests in order to reach full compatibility.
